# Pedagogy review — 25-deep-learning-lenses

**Verdict: REVISE** (light — one structural fix + two mediums; no correctness defects, harness green, all prose numbers ⊆ printed output).

Reviewed as an ML engineer who ships nets and has heard every one of these as slogans. The module is genuinely strong: dense, honest, scope-disciplined (exactly 2 labs + 2 exact demos + ledger + the mandated theorems-first, no smuggled extra "territory"), every callback (M01/05/07/12/13/14/15/18) load-bearing and specific, and the honesty labels do the anti-triumphalist work the brief demands. The one thing that blocks approval is that the module's *named deliverable* — the triage skill — is the one thing the reader never actually practices.

- **Double descent** pays off as "flat-prior blow-up cured by a prior" verbatim; ridge-erases-the-spike + effective-parameters reading + deep-net **Open** label all present.

- **Non-tribalism**: Open rows admitted (ICL, cold posteriors, overparameterization Theorem/**Open**); "merely rhymes," "do not oversell it," Theorem-for-sampler-only — the ledger reads as triage, not annexation.
