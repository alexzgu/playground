235. A particularly infamous example of an un-replicable economic finding that had a big impact on policy is
Reinhart and Rogoff (2010). Although apparently, if not actually, influential in national and international bud-

in the data, remember. But if you torture the data long enough, it will confess. [566]

243. Two excellent examples of this phenomenon occurred in 2014 and 2015. First, Lin et al. (2014) published an

The original authors continue to deny the results were in error, and the saga continues. The second involves a
competition held by Lior Pachtor on his blog: https://liorpachter.wordpress.com/2015/05/26/pachters-p-value-
prize/. I recommend reading the whole thing, including the comments, which is where the action is. [567]
