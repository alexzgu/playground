Despite its slight disadvantage in accuracy, PSIS has a distinct advantage in warning
the user about when it is unreliable. The k values that PSIS computes for each observation
indicate when the PSIS score may be unreliable, as well as identify which observations are at
fault. We’ll see later how useful this can be.
