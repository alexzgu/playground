218. The moralizing gods data were originally published in Nature, which is a scientific publication of some
prestige, and the authors did replace every NA with zero. The paper itself never mentions missing data in the
moralizing gods variable, so it probably wasn’t noticed during peer review. But because the authors were respon-
sible and provided all the data and analysis code, several people independently noticed the NA-to-zero issue
after publication. The line of code is obvious: https://github.com/pesavage/moralizing-gods/commit/ff61f9c.
The authors deserve much credit for their transparency. For the record, the original authors still don’t agree that
replacing NA with zero was wrong. You can read the original report, the criticisms, and the authors responses for
yourself: Whitehouse et al. (2019), Beheim et al. (2019), Savage et al. (2019). Note that there are two analyses in
the original paper and both treat NA as zero. [528]
